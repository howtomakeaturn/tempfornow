Atayal

Atayal is a special CMS dropping a lot of conventions used by other CMS platforms. (Here's why)

Atayal is a CMS composed by just several Laravel Packages.

Why?

Because Laravel itself is flexible enough, we shouldn't make more magics on top of it.

Just utilize what Laravel provides.

To extend Atayal should be just dragging and dropping your Laravel Packages to 'Atayal/Modules/' folder.
